Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-08

Vinylkeep is an offline-first app. All data you enter — including record entries, notes, and dates — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data, no analytics, and no usage tracking. Vinylkeep does not use the network for any purpose other than processing in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit, which is handled entirely by Apple.

Data Storage

Your entries are saved in a local JSON file within the app's Application Support directory. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it. We do not have access to it.

Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

Vinylkeep does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.
